Compliance Deadlines
Key dates under the Malawi Data Protection Act 2024
⏰ Important Dates
🚨
December 2024 — Major Importance Deadline (PASSED)
Organizations of major importance (banks, telecoms, hospitals, government) must have been fully compliant by December 2024 (6 months from commencement). If this applies to your organization, you are already at risk of enforcement action.
⚠️
June 2026 — All Organizations Deadline
All ordinary data controllers and processors must be fully compliant by June 2026 (2 years from commencement on 3 June 2024). Time to act is now — implementation takes months.
⏱️
72 Hours — Breach Notification Deadline (Ongoing)
From the moment you discover a data breach, you have 72 hours to notify MACRA. This obligation is active NOW, regardless of your compliance deadline.
📋
Before Processing — DPIA Submission
Data Protection Impact Assessments for high-risk activities must be submitted to MACRA BEFORE the processing activity begins. Retroactive DPIAs are not compliant.
