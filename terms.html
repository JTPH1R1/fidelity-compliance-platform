🛠️ 2. Description of the Service

Fidelity Insurance Assessors provides a digital compliance audit platform designed to help organizations in Malawi assess and improve their compliance posture under the Malawi Data Protection Act 2024.

What the Service Provides

  • A structured 12-section, 65-question compliance audit checklist aligned to the DPA 2024
  • A weighted scoring engine that calculates your organization's compliance score and grade
  • Detailed Audit Reports with section breakdowns, critical flags, and improvement recommendations
  • Plain-English explanations of all DPA 2024 obligations with references to the relevant Act sections
  • Printable reports suitable for internal governance, board reporting, and MACRA engagement
  • Auto-save functionality to preserve audit progress between sessions

What the Service Does Not Provide

  • Legal advice — the Platform is an informational and assessment tool, not a law firm or legal adviser
  • Regulatory certification — completing an audit on this platform does not constitute certification by MACRA or the Data Protection Commissioner
  • A guarantee of regulatory compliance or immunity from investigation
  • DPA 2024 registration services — you must register directly with MACRA

We reserve the right to modify, update, or discontinue features of the Service with reasonable notice to registered users.

📊 5. Audit Results and Compliance Grades

The Platform uses a weighted scoring algorithm based on the 12 compliance areas defined in the DPA 2024. Your Audit Report includes a compliance score and one of five grade classifications.

Nature of Audit Results

Audit Results produced by this Platform are:

  • Self-assessments — based entirely on your own answers. Results are as accurate as the information you provide.
  • Guidance tools — designed to help your organization understand its compliance gaps and prioritize improvements.
  • Point-in-time assessments — reflecting the state of your organization's compliance at the time the audit was completed. Compliance positions change as your practices and the regulatory environment evolve.

What Audit Results Are NOT

  • A formal compliance certification issued by MACRA or the Data Protection Commissioner
  • A legal opinion on your organization's compliance status
  • A guarantee against investigation, enforcement action, or penalties under the DPA 2024
  • A substitute for independent legal or professional compliance advice

Fidelity Insurance Assessors offers professional compliance consultancy services to organizations seeking a formal, independently assessed compliance posture. Contact us at info@fidelityassessors.mw to enquire about our assessment services.
