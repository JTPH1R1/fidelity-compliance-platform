The Law You Must Follow

Malawi Data Protection Act 2024

Malawi's first comprehensive data protection law regulates how organizations collect, store, use, and share personal information. It grants individuals rights over their data and imposes binding obligations on organizations.

Key Compliance Milestones

✓
3 June 2024
DPA 2024 Comes Into Force
The Act is officially enacted. All organizations processing personal data become subject to its provisions.
⚡
December 2024
Major Importance Deadline
Organizations of "major importance" must be fully compliant (6-month window). Banks, telecoms, hospitals, government entities.
⏰
June 2026
All Organizations Deadline
All ordinary data controllers and processors must be fully compliant (2-year window).
📋
Ongoing
MACRA Enforcement & Audits
MACRA has the power to investigate, audit, and impose compliance orders and fines at any time.
